FAQ

The questions that decide whether someone installs or scrolls away.

Does NeverMiss read my email content?

NeverMiss is designed to minimize data. It operates inside Gmail to power follow-ups and signals. See the Privacy page for specifics and constraints.

Will tracking hurt deliverability?

Deliverability can be affected by many factors. NeverMiss aims to be lightweight. If you have strict deliverability concerns, test with a small subset first.

Does it work with Google Workspace Gmail?

If you can use Gmail in Chrome, it should generally work. Some org policies can restrict extensions.

Do I need a CRM?

No. NeverMiss is intentionally not a CRM. It's a follow-up command center inside Gmail.

What happens after the 30-day trial?

You keep Free forever. If you're hitting limits because you're actually using it, Pro is there.

How do I uninstall?

Remove it from Chrome extensions. You can also review data handling on the Privacy page.

Why this exists

This was built because a real operator needed it. Deals don't die in dramatic explosions. They die in silence. NeverMiss exists to stop that, inside the inbox where work actually happens.
