A faster Streak alternative that doesn't slow down Gmail

Streak pioneered the idea of a CRM inside Gmail, and that idea is right. But if you've been using Streak for a while, you know the tradeoffs: Gmail gets noticeably slower, the free tier keeps shrinking, and it tries to be a full CRM — pipelines, mail merge, reporting — when all you really need is follow-up discipline.

NeverMiss takes the same "inside Gmail" approach, but stays deliberately lightweight.

Common reasons people leave Streak

  • Performance. Streak adds weight to every Gmail page load. The more data in your pipelines, the slower it gets.
  • Pricing changes. The free plan that once included pipelines and tracking has been steadily cut back. Solo users now need the $49/month plan for basic features.
  • Feature sprawl. Mail merge, shared pipelines, thread sharing, snippets — most solo sellers use maybe 20% of what Streak offers.
  • Stale data. Streak gives you a pipeline, but doesn't proactively tell you when deals go cold. You have to notice yourself.

Who should stay on Streak

If you rely on Streak's mail merge, shared pipelines for a team of 10+, or deep reporting — it's still the most full-featured Gmail CRM. Streak is a CRM that happens to live in Gmail.

Who NeverMiss is for

Solo operators and small teams who don't need a CRM — they need to stop forgetting follow-ups. NeverMiss is a follow-up command center, not a CRM. It does five things well and nothing else.
