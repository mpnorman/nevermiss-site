Alternative

A simpler HubSpot alternative that lives inside Gmail

HubSpot is a powerful platform. But if you're a solo operator or small team selling from Gmail, you've probably noticed the gap between HubSpot's promise and your daily reality: a separate tab you forget to check, a CRM that takes longer to update than the email itself, and pricing that quietly climbs as you hit feature gates.

You don't need a marketing suite. You need your follow-ups to stop slipping.

Where HubSpot gets heavy

  • The free tier is a funnel. You get basic contacts, but follow-up sequences, open tracking beyond a handful, and pipeline automation are paywalled — often at $45–90/user/month.
  • Context-switching kills momentum. HubSpot lives in its own tab. Every time you leave Gmail to log an activity, you break your flow.
  • It's built for teams with a sales ops person. If nobody configures the deal stages, workflows, and reporting dashboards, the CRM just collects dust.
  • Data entry becomes the job. Solo sellers end up spending more time feeding the CRM than closing deals.

What NeverMiss does instead

NeverMiss is a Chrome extension that turns Gmail into a follow-up command center. No separate tab. No CRM to maintain. Just the follow-up discipline you need, right where your conversations happen.

Who should stay on HubSpot

If you have a dedicated sales ops team, need marketing automation, or manage 50+ reps with complex reporting — HubSpot is the right tool. It's genuinely great at enterprise scale.

Who NeverMiss is built for

Solo operators, freelancers, consultants, and small teams (under 10 people) who sell from Gmail and need follow-up discipline without a full CRM. If you've ever thought "I'll follow up tomorrow" and then didn't — that's the problem NeverMiss solves.
