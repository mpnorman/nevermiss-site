Alternative

A Pipedrive alternative for people who live in Gmail

Pipedrive is a great visual pipeline tool. The drag-and-drop board is genuinely satisfying. But here's what happens in practice: you send an email in Gmail, then switch tabs to Pipedrive to move a card, log an activity, and update the deal stage. Then you switch back to Gmail. Repeat 30 times a day.

If most of your selling happens in email, the CRM should live where the email lives.

Where Pipedrive creates friction

  • Tab-switching tax. Every deal update requires leaving Gmail. Over a day, this adds up to significant lost focus and momentum.
  • Add-on paywalls. Email tracking, workflow automations, and advanced reporting are paid add-ons on top of the base price. The $14/month plan quickly becomes $40+.
  • Pipeline without follow-up discipline. Pipedrive shows you where deals are, but doesn't proactively nudge you when a deal goes stale. You have to notice the silence yourself.
  • Built for pipeline managers, not inbox workers. If your daily workflow is "open Gmail, send emails, close deals," Pipedrive asks you to adopt a second workflow in a second app.

Who should stay on Pipedrive

Teams that need shared pipelines with multiple reps, visual deal boards for pipeline review meetings, or integrations with calling tools and marketing platforms. Pipedrive is a purpose-built sales CRM and it does that job well.

Who NeverMiss is for

Solo sellers and small teams who do 90% of their work in Gmail and want follow-up discipline without maintaining a separate app. If your pipeline has fewer than 50 active deals and you'd rather work from your inbox than a dashboard, NeverMiss is built for you.
